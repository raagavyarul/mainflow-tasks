About Us

Welcome to The Cake Cave! A bakery born out of passion and determination by an 18-year-old entrepreneur. Our founder, Raagavy began their journey in the kitchen, perfecting recipes and dreaming of opening a place where everyone could enjoy fresh, delicious baked goods.

At The Cake Cave, we believe in the magic of homemade cakes and pastries that make every moment sweeter. Every treat we create is baked with love, using the finest ingredients, and crafted with care.

As a startup, we’re excited to grow and share our creations with the community. We’re dedicated to bringing a bit of sweetness into your life, one bite at a time.
